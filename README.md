## Ethics Statement
  I believe that technology and artificial intelligence should be developed responsibly and used in ways that benefit society. When building software    or working with data, I aim to follow several core principles:
  - Respect privacy - Personal data should be collected and used only when necessary, handled securely, and never misused.
  - Promote fairness - Algorithms and AI should be designed with awareness of potential bias and should be evaluated to ensure they do not                 unfairly disadvantage certain groups.
  - Be transparent - When possible, programs and applications should be understandable in their intentions and not maliciously mislead users.
  - Take responsibility - Developers should consider the real-world impact of their work and be accountable for how their systems are used.

  As a developer, I strive to build technology that is ethical, reliable, and respectful of the people who interact with it.
